About Us

First Street Chick Enterprise is a family-owned, locally-sourced chicken farm located in Kabarnet, Kenya. We specialize in providing fresh, high-quality chicken products to our customers, offering a variety of meat options, such as breasts, thighs, and drumsticks.

Our farmers and employees are committed to creating a culture of love, respect, and sustainability. We strive to provide our customers with the best possible chicken products.
